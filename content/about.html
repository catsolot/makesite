About

Pulpways is a blog for anything and everything that I don't want directly associated with my professional identity. Mostly posts about arts and culture, but maybe some other things. Perhaps random thoughts.

Why Pulpways?

The name Pulpways comes from a combination of wood pulp (and pulp magazines) and Folkways, the folk music record label. I thought it sounded cool, which is what really matters.
